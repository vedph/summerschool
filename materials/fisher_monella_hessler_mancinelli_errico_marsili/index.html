Bodl. 264)](https://iiif.bodleian.ox.ac.uk/iiif/viewer/d21e23ac-11a8-4d24-ad4d-07ad10e0ea34/#?c=0&m=0&s=0&cv=456&r=0&xywh=-4578%2C-380%2C14494%2C7574) (M.V. Errico, S. Marsili, 15') - **Prototype** Click [here](https://github.com/vedph/summer-globe-22) and play with the digital edition prototype of the manuscript (5') - **Discussion**. Look at the manuscript facsimile and its transcription: what elements would *you* include in a digital representation, and why? (P. Monella, 15 minutes) - **Code reading and discussion** Course attendants hack (read) the digital edition TEI source code, and get an inductive introduction to the basics of XML markup. Part 1: material structure *vs* textual structure - headers, paragraphs, line breaks, rubrica etc. (H. Essler, 15') - **Code reading and discussion** Part 2: named entities and Linked Open Data - people and place names (T. Mancinelli, 20') - **Round up/Wrap up/Q&A** (F. Fischer, 5')
