"Leaving Venice" session working materials

Session led by Franz Fischer (Ca’ Foscari), Paolo Monella (Sapienza University of Rome), Holger Hessler (Ca’ Foscari), Tiziana Mancinelli (Ca’ Foscari; ICDP Digital Library).

Outline

  • Introduction The Milione and the Bodleian Library manuscript (MS. Bodl. 264) (M.V. Errico, S. Marsili, 15’)
  • Prototype Click here and play with the digital edition prototype of the manuscript (5’)
  • Discussion. Look at the manuscript facsimile and its transcription: what elements would you include in a digital representation, and why? (P. Monella, 15 minutes)
  • Code reading and discussion Course attendants hack (read) the digital edition TEI source code, and get an inductive introduction to the basics of XML markup. Part 1: material structure vs textual structure - headers, paragraphs, line breaks, rubrica etc. (H. Essler, 15’)
  • Code reading and discussion Part 2: named entities and Linked Open Data - people and place names (T. Mancinelli, 20’)
  • Round up/Wrap up/Q&A (F. Fischer, 5’)
